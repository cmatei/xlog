Appendix A - Supported Modes

Xlog uses different mode values to provide detailed logging for contacts. There are several different ways to define each mode. Enter the one of the values in the "Values" field below into the logg defaults.

Continuous Wave (MODE_CW)
Values: CW, A1, A2

Single Sideband (MODE_SSB)
Values: SSB, USB, LSB, J3E, A3J, R3E, H3E, A3R, A3H

Radio TeleType (MODE_RTTY)
Values: PSK31, PSK, BPSK, BPSK31, QPSK

Slow Scan Television (MODE_SSTV)
Values: SSTV

Multi-frequency Shift Keyed, Symbol Rate 7.8125 Hz (MODE_MFSK8)
Values: MFSK8

Multi-frequency Shift Keyed, Symbol Rate 15.625 Hz (MODE_MFSK160)
Values: MFSK16

Phase Shift Keying at a rate of 63 baud (MODE_PSK63)
Values: PSK63, BPSK63

Phase Shift Keying at a rate of 125 baud (MODE_PSK125)
Values: PSK125

Frequency Modulation (MODE_FM)
Values: FM, F3, 20K0F3E

Amplitude Modulation (MODE_AM)
Values: AM, A3E, A3

ASCII (MODE_ASCII)
Values: ASCI, ASCII

Amateur TV (MODE_ATV)
Values: ATV, A5, C3F

Clover (MODE_CLO)
Values: CLO

Facsimile (MODE_FAX)
Values: FAX

Golay-TOR (MODE_GTOR)
Values: GTOR

Hellschreiber (MODE_HELL)
